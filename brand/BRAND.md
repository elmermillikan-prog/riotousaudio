# Riotous Audio — Brand Aesthetic Brief

Reference for logo / identity design. Auto-derived from the production stylesheet
(riotousaudio.com/assets/site.css), whose own header calls the system
"Audiophile Gallery / Italian Atelier." Last updated 2026-06-06.

ONE-LINE VIBE: A dark, hushed luxury gallery — antique gold on near-black,
elegant Italian-serif headlines, hairline detailing. High-end audiophile
showroom / Italian atelier — NOT "car stereo shop."

  Signature gold      #c9a86a   muted antique/champagne gold — the brand color (NOT bright/chrome)
  Gold bright         #e3c489   highlights, hover
  Gold deep           #9c804a   shadow/engraved tone
  Near-black base     #0a0a0c   primary background
  Off-black panels    #101014 / #16161c   cards, layered surfaces
  Warm ivory (text)   #e9e6df   main text — warm white, not pure white
  Soft taupe          #b6b1a6   secondary text
  Faint stone         #6f6a60   muted / fine print
  Steel blue accent   #3f74a8   used VERY sparingly, only as faint atmospheric glow
  -> Whole site = warm metal on near-black. Gold + ivory are the only real colors.

TYPOGRAPHY
  Display/headlines/product names: Cormorant Garamond (high-contrast elegant serif,
    light weights 300-400, italic for emphasis words). The soul of the brand.
  Labels/body/buttons: Archivo (clean modern sans), UPPERCASE + wide letter-spacing
    (0.16-0.34em) for small "eyebrow" labels.
  Signature = refined serif headline + spaced-out uppercase sans label.

VISUAL MOTIFS & FEEL
  - The dot: wordmark "Riotous"(ivory) + "Audio"(gold) preceded by a small glowing
    gold dot (6px, gold halo). Closest existing mark — worth building on.
  - Soft gold halos behind floating products (single-spotlight feel).
  - Barely-there (~2.5%) film grain over everything — analog/tactile warmth.
  - Hairline borders (1px, 8-18% opacity), corners barely rounded (2-6px). Restrained.
  - Em-dash (—) bullets in gold. Editorial/magazine.
  - Voice: "pursuit of perfect sound", "Three Houses One Standard", brands as
    "marques"/"houses", "an exercise in obsession". Heritage luxury, quiet confidence.

LOGO DIRECTION
  - Wordmark or "RA" monogram in a high-contrast serif (Cormorant-like) fits best.
    A refined emblem (concentric ring/halo, single dot, abstract soundwave) echoes
    the dot/halo motif.
  - Antique gold #c9a86a on #0a0a0c is the signature combo. Supply gold-on-dark AND
    a reversed (dark on light/gold) version.
  - Must survive at favicon size (16-32px) — favor a simple mark over fine detail.
  - AVOID: bright/primary colors, neon, chrome gradients, bold geometric "tech"/"racing"
    styling, anything that reads cheap car-audio. Bar is jewelry/fashion-house.

DELIVERABLES MOST USEFUL
  1. Primary logo — gold on near-black (#c9a86a / #0a0a0c)
  2. Reversed logo — for light backgrounds
  3. Simplified mark/monogram readable at 16-32px (drives favicon + app icon)
  4. Vector source (SVG/AI) + transparent PNGs

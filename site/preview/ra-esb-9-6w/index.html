ESB · 9000 Series

9.6W

6.5″/165 mm Mid-Woofer

$1,799.99MSRP · USD

Some of the most technologically advanced car speakers ever produced. The 9000 Series mid-woofer pairs a high-modulus carbon-fibre cone — molded under vacuum and autoclave — with an oversized neodymium magnet and an FEM-analysed cast-aluminium basket, delivering naturalness of reproduction with explosive transient dynamics.

Main Features

  • The pole cup, designed with magnetic-flux analysis software, achieves perfect, homogeneous saturation of the magnetic gap — keeping the voice coil immersed in constant magnetic flux throughout its stroke.
  • A very powerful, high-temperature-resistant neodymium magnet greatly reduces the size and bulk of the magnetic motor.
  • The cast-aluminium, powder-coated basket offers very low resistance to airflow; its FEM-verified shape reduces vibration and resonance.
  • The 75 mm super-light voice coil ensures highest-in-class power handling and unequalled dynamics, staying fully immersed in the magnetic field through much of its excursion to minimise distortion. Its aluminium former has ventilation holes and black anodisation to better dissipate heat.
  • The cone is high-modulus carbon, molded under vacuum and autoclave, for naturalness of reproduction and high transient dynamics.
  • The NBL rubber surround is shaped for very smooth movement at low excursion, becoming increasingly controlled as excursion rises — making the speaker extremely dynamic and able to handle large power.
  • A CNC aluminium ring secures the rubber suspension at the edge for perfect fastening and centering.

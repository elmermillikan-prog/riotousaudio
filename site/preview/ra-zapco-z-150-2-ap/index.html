Zapco began its quest for perfect sonic reproduction in 1974. The Z-AP “Audiophile” Series is the answer for the car — an amplifier built to set new sound-quality standards for both home and car, designed by listening as much as by measuring.

The AP Difference

  • The same high-end audiophile electrolytic caps in the power supply as Zapco's flagship LX amps, with WIMA poly caps in the signal path.
  • A new low-noise audio op-amp at the input stage and the smoothest available op-amp in the signal path — the right op-amp for the right job.
  • Doubled output drivers with increased operating voltage on matched final devices; a new aerospace insulator pulls heat off the outputs faster for higher efficiency.
  • 0.1% precision resistors at the differential input stage (not 1%) for the lowest possible noise floor.
  • Patented multi-ground RCA connectors with extra-heavy gold plating for perfect signal transfer.
  • Low-noise gain pots with detents for easy, repeatable level setting — finished in matt black with an engraved solid-copper serial plate.

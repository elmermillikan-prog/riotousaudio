A6 Front 200

2-Way Front System for Audi A6

$949.99MSRP · USD

The A6FRONT200 is an ESB 6000 Car Special Series speaker — conceived and built in Italy as an exercise in obsession.

The A6 Front 200 is a plug-and-play 2-way front system built specifically for the Audi A6 — an 8"/200 mm ESB A6-F81 woofer with a double-neodymium motor and a matched dome tweeter, mounted on a die-cast aluminum Audi adapter that drops straight into the factory door location. No fabrication, no compromise.

Main Features

  • Double neodymium N42 motor for high efficiency and linear control across the full voice-coil excursion
  • PP-TD20 cone — mineral-filled polypropylene for rigidity, low mass and clean transient response
  • 32.5 mm 4-layer aluminum voice coil on an aluminum 3003-H18 former for extended power handling
  • Die-cast aluminum frame with a long-excursion rubber surround
  • Die-cast aluminum Audi adapter with OE-matching connectors — true plug & play, no modifications

MB Front 100

2-Way Front System for Mercedes-Benz

$329.99MSRP · USD

The MB Front 100 is a plug-and-play 2-way front speaker system engineered specifically for many Mercedes-Benz vehicles, pairing a 4"/100 mm carbon fiber woofer with a 1"/25 mm silk dome tweeter and a dedicated crossover.

Main Features

  • Neodymium magnet ensures greater efficiency and improves linearity along the voice coil's excursion
  • Carbon fiber cone delivers extraordinary rigidity with very low weight for high transient dynamics
  • 25 mm silk dome tweeter for a smooth, linear, very extended high-frequency response
  • Plug & Play — special adaptor fits many Mercedes-Benz vehicles with no modifications

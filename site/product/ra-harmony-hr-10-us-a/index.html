Harmony · Hard Rock Series

HR 10 US-A

10”/250 mm Underseat Act. Sub for Android

$399.99MSRP · USD

The HR 10 US-A is a 5-channel Class-AB amplifier and active subwoofer system for Android head units, delivering 4 × 70 W + 400 W max (4 × 35 W + 200 W RMS) in an aluminum underseat enclosure. It includes an Android Plug&Play connection cable and a 4-band EQ, allowing the entire audio system to be installed under the front seat with no additional wiring beyond the supplied harness. A 0–12 dB bass boost and 0°–180° phase control are built in.

Key Features

  • 5-channel Class-AB amplifier: 4 × 35 W RMS front/rear + 200 W RMS subwoofer (200 W RMS / 400 W max sub channel)
  • Android Plug&Play connection cable — no additional wiring beyond supplied harness
  • 4-band EQ with −9 / +9 dB range
  • Low-pass crossover adjustable from 50 Hz to 150 Hz with 0 – 12 dB bass boost
  • 0° – 180° phase control and included wired remote control
  • Compact aluminum underseat enclosure (34.5 × 26.1 × 7.8 cm)

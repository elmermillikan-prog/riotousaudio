ADSP-Z12 IV-10A

Power 2Ω: 10 x 120W, 4Ω: 10 x 80W

$1,299.99MSRP · USD

The ADSP-Z12 IV-10A is an integrated 12-channel DSP and 10-channel amplifier delivering 10 × 120 W at 2 Ω or 10 × 80 W at 4 Ω. The CS47048 32-bit/192 kHz DSP core provides 31-band graphic/parametric equalization, four crossover filter types, and time alignment across 12 processing channels. Coaxial and optical digital inputs, 8-channel speaker-level inputs with auto-on, and a port for the optional HD-BT module round out the connectivity.

Key Features

  • 10-channel amplifier: 10 × 120 W @ 2 Ω / 10 × 80 W @ 4 Ω
  • CS47048 32-bit/192 kHz DSP with THD+N −98 dB
  • 31-band graphic/parametric EQ with Linkwitz, Butterworth, Bessel, Tchebyschev crossovers
  • Coaxial (32-bit/192 kHz) and optical (24-bit/192 kHz) digital inputs
  • 8-channel speaker-level inputs (2–20 V) with auto-on
  • Port for optional HD-BT II-D module — aptX / aptX HD Bluetooth streaming

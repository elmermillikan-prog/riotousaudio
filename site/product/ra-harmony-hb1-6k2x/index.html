Harmony · Hard Rock Series

HB1.6K2X

2-Way Speaker System with 12dB Crossover

$139.99MSRP · USD

The HR 6K2X is a 6.5"/165 mm 2-way component speaker system with a 0.8"/20 mm silk dome tweeter and a 12 dB in-line tweeter crossover. It handles 60 W RMS (120 W transient) with a nominal impedance of 4 Ω and a frequency response of 65 Hz – 20 kHz. The woofer features a pressed paper cone, aluminum voice coil support, rubber surround, and a high-grade ferrite magnet motor.

Key Features

  • 6.5"/165 mm paper-cone mid-woofer with aluminum voice coil support for improved thermal dissipation
  • 0.8"/20 mm silk dome tweeter with high-grade neodymium magnet for extended high-frequency response
  • 12 dB in-line tweeter crossover built to handle higher power than the speakers
  • Aerodynamically designed open basket optimizes ventilation and decompression
  • Rubber surround for high damping and transversal stability
  • Compatible with factory head units or aftermarket amplifiers

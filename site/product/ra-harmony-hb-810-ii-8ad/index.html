HB 810 II 8AD

10-Ch. DSP/4(AB)+4(D)-Ch. Amp, BT & Player N/A N/A N/A

$749.99MSRP · USD

The HB 810 II 8AD is a 10-channel DSP with an integrated 8-channel hybrid amplifier section combining 4-channel Class AB and 4-channel bridgeable Class D stages. The Class AB channels deliver 4 × 35 W (4 Ω) / 4 × 50 W (2 Ω) RMS, while the Class D channels deliver 4 × 100 W (4 Ω) / 4 × 150 W (2 Ω) / 2 × 300 W (4 Ω bridged) RMS. It includes 31-band EQ, HD Bluetooth streaming, integrated Music Player, optical and OTG inputs, and full PC and mobile DSP control.

Key Features

  • 10-channel DSP with 4-Ch Class AB + 4-Ch bridgeable Class D amp
  • Class AB RMS: 4 × 35 W (4 Ω) / 4 × 50 W (2 Ω)
  • Class D RMS: 4 × 100 W (4 Ω) / 4 × 150 W (2 Ω) / 2 × 300 W (4 Ω br.)
  • 31-band EQ per output channel with I/O Mixing Matrix
  • Optical digital input plus Aux, BT, High-Level, USB, OTG sources
  • HD Bluetooth streaming, integrated Music Player, PC and mobile APP control

A4 Front 200

2-Way Front System for Audi A4

$899.99MSRP · USD

The A4FRONT200 is an ESB 6000 Car Special Series speaker — conceived and built in Italy as an exercise in obsession.

The A4 Front 200 is a plug-and-play 2-way front system built specifically for the Audi A4 — a 8"/200 mm neodymium woofer and a matched dome tweeter on a shallow-mount frame that drops straight into the factory door location using ESB’s vehicle-specific adapters. No fabrication, no compromise.

Main Features

  • Neodymium magnet for greater efficiency and improved linearity across the voice coil’s excursion
  • 8"/200 mm woofer delivers deep, effortless mid-bass from the factory door location
  • Matched dome tweeter for a smooth, linear, very extended high-frequency response
  • Shallow-depth design fits tight factory cavities with no modifications
  • Plug & Play — anodized aluminum adapters and OE-matching connectors fit the Audi A4 directly

Z-OTG-BT C

20 N/A

$199.99MSRP · USD

The Z-OTG-BT C is a high-quality audio converter combining a USB OTG input (192 kHz/32-bit) with Qualcomm Bluetooth 5.2 (supporting aptX, aptX HD, and aptX Adaptive at 192 kHz/24-bit) and a TI PCM5102A DAC running at up to 384 kHz/32-bit. It outputs simultaneously to optical and coaxial digital outputs (compatible with 48/96/192 kHz digital inputs) and a stereo RCA pair at 1.5 V RMS, powered from a standard 9 – 16 V DC supply.

Key Features

  • USB OTG input at 192 kHz/32-bit with device charging function
  • Qualcomm Bluetooth 5.2 with aptX, aptX HD, and aptX Adaptive at 192 kHz/24-bit
  • TI PCM5102A DAC capable of 384 kHz/32-bit conversion
  • JRC 5532 high-performance, low-noise dual op-amp in the signal path
  • Simultaneous optical and coaxial digital outputs (48/96/192 kHz compatible)
  • Stereo RCA outputs at 1.5 V RMS; powered from 9 – 16 V DC

Zapco · Analog Processors Series

ASP-X4

Crossover

$399.99MSRP · USD

The ASP-X4 is a 2/3/4-way electronic crossover with a 9.5 V line driver built in, accepting 4 channels of input and delivering up to 8 channels of output — including 6 main channels plus a dedicated 2-channel subwoofer output. Its versatile top-panel controls let users reconfigure it as a 2-way, 3-way, or 4-way crossover to suit virtually any system layout, with a switchable 12/24 dB slope and a dash-mountable remote for sub level control.

Key Features

  • Configurable as 2-way, 3-way, or 4-way electronic crossover from a single unit
  • 4-channel inputs (Rear + Front) driving 8 channels of output (6 main + 2 sub)
  • 9.5 V RMS line driver output voltage — far above typical head-unit preamp levels
  • HP 1 filter 500 Hz – 5 kHz; BP 2 filter 50 Hz – 5 kHz; BP 3 filter 50 Hz – 3 kHz; LP 4 filter 45 Hz – 450 Hz
  • Subsonic filter adjustable 15 Hz – 60 Hz; selectable 12/24 dB slope
  • Dash remote control for channels 7/8 (sub); includes remote power supply

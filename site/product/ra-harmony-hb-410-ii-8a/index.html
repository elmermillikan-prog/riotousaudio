HB 410 II 8A

10-Ch. DSP/8-Ch. Class AB Amp, BT & Player

$499.99MSRP · USD

The HB 410 II 8A is a 10-channel DSP combined with an integrated 8-channel Class AB amplifier, delivering 8 × 23 W RMS at 4 Ω and 8 × 35 W RMS at 2 Ω (8 × 70 W Max). It features 31-band equalization, HD Bluetooth streaming, an integrated Music Player, OTG input, and full PC and mobile control via a dedicated app. The optional HB DSP II-R remote provides dashboard-level control of volume, presets, source selection, and Music Player navigation.

Key Features

  • 10-channel DSP with integrated 8-Ch Class AB amplifier
  • 31-band EQ per output channel with I/O Mixing Matrix
  • RMS: 8 × 23 W (4 Ω) / 8 × 35 W (2 Ω); 8 × 70 W Max
  • HD Bluetooth streaming and integrated Music Player (App)
  • 2-Ch RCA inputs, 4-Ch high-level inputs, 10-Ch RCA outputs
  • PC and mobile APP DSP control; optional remote for dashboard use

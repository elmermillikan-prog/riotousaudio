Harmony · Blue Note Series

HB 68 II

8-Ch. DSP, BT & Player

$349.99MSRP · USD

The HB 68 II is a pure 8-channel DSP with 31-band equalization and a full suite of control features including I/O Matrix, LP and HP Filters, Speaker Assignment, Delay, Levels, and Polarity. It features HD Bluetooth streaming, an integrated Music Player with OTG input, and supports both PC and mobile (smartphone) control via a dedicated app. The optional HB DSP II-R remote allows volume, preset, source, and song-navigation control from the dashboard.

Key Features

  • 8-channel DSP with 31-band EQ per output channel
  • HD Bluetooth streaming and integrated Music Player (App)
  • 2-Ch RCA inputs, 6-Ch high-level inputs, 8-Ch RCA outputs
  • I/O Mixing Matrix with Butterworth, Bessel, and Linkwitz crossover types
  • Crossover slopes 6 – 48 dB/Oct., PC and mobile APP control
  • Compatible with optional HB DSP II-R remote for volume, preset, and source selection

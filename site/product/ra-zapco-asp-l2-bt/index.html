Zapco · Analog Processors Series

ASP-L2 BT

Line Driver

$199.99MSRP · USD

The ASP-L2 BT is a 2-channel, 9.5 V line driver with Bluetooth streaming, delivering 24 dB of gain across 4 output channels so a single source can drive two amplifiers without Y-adapters. It accepts a direct aux connection from a smart device or MP3 player — ideal for classic cars, motorcycles, or side-by-sides — and includes a dash remote volume control. Elna Silmic II capacitors and Analog Devices OP 275 op-amps hold THD + Noise below 0.01%.

Key Features

  • 2-channel line driver with Bluetooth streaming and 4 RCA outputs at 9.5 V RMS each
  • 24 dB of signal gain eliminates the need to over-drive amplifier input gain
  • Direct aux input from smart device or MP3 player — no head unit required
  • Two sets of preamp outputs allow driving separate main and bass amplifiers without Y-adapters
  • Dash remote volume control included
  • THD + Noise below 0.01% using Elna Silmic II caps and OP 275 op-amps

HB1.6C

6.5”/165 mm Coaxial

$109.99MSRP · USD

The HR 6C is a 6.5"/165 mm coaxial speaker with an integrated 0.51"/13 mm mylar dome tweeter. It handles 50 W RMS (100 W transient) with a nominal impedance of 4 Ω and a frequency response of 65 Hz – 18 kHz. Designed for direct replacement in factory speaker housings, it pairs easily with OEM or aftermarket head units.

Key Features

  • 6.5"/165 mm paper-cone woofer with aluminum voice coil support
  • Integrated 0.51"/13 mm mylar dome tweeter with neodymium magnet
  • Drop-in coaxial design fits original factory speaker housings
  • Rubber surround for high damping and transversal stability
  • Compatible with factory head units or aftermarket amplifiers

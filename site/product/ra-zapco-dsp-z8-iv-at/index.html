DSP-Z8 IV AT

DSP, 8-Ch, optional HD-BT 4 9,2 0,032

$599.99MSRP · USD

The DSP-Z8 IV AT is an 8-channel standalone digital signal processor featuring a CS47048 32-bit/192 kHz DSP and auto-calibration (AutoTune) with optional microphone. It accepts up to 8 channels of speaker-level or RCA analog input plus coaxial and optical digital inputs, and outputs 8 channels of 5 V RMS RCA signal. A port for the optional HD-BT module adds aptX HD Bluetooth streaming, and the included LCD dash remote provides source, volume, and bass control.

Key Features

  • CS47048 32-bit/192 kHz DSP with THD+N −98 dB
  • AutoTune auto-calibration with optional M-AT1 calibration microphone
  • Coaxial (32-bit/192 kHz) and optical (24-bit/192 kHz) digital inputs
  • 8-channel speaker-level inputs (2–20 V) with auto-on
  • Port for optional HD-BT II-D module — aptX / aptX HD Bluetooth streaming
  • Included 1" LCD dash remote with source, volume, and bass output control

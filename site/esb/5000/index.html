ESB

5000 Series

Elegance and technology — premium systems that make a bold statement and blend with any interior.

The mission of this project was to create a loudspeaker system with extremely high transient response, for music reproduction with more realistic impact and involvement. The main goal was to reduce moving masses, making the mobile part of the loudspeaker lighter and faster.

Great attention was paid to axial and radial ventilation of the voice coil. Metal parts are CNC machined for maximum magnetic flux linearity and minimum loss. The carbon-fiber-reinforced composite cone/dome ensures extraordinary rigidity combined with very low weight.

ESB

1000 Series

The ESB sound in affordable speakers, without sacrificing quality.

The 1000 Series was conceived to offer ESB sound in low-cost speakers without sacrificing quality. The woofer's pressed paper cone, large voice coils for high power, and silk dome tweeters with neodymium magnets are just some of the features that make these speakers uncompromising.

Each system is designed to be easily installed in a vehicle's original speaker housings and integrated with a factory head unit or an aftermarket amplifier, whether the vehicle uses coaxial or two-way speaker systems. Particular attention was given to mounting depth, which at just 59 mm is among the shallowest on the market today.

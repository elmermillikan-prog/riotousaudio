ESB

3000 Series

A complete, modular speaker range — the broadest expression of the ESB sound.

The ESB 3000 speaker system was developed and assembled with a great commitment to the research of materials and engineering solutions of the highest level.

The baskets are engineered for maximum torsional rigidity. The cones use pressed paper with a waterproof treatment — an ideal marriage of lightness, stiffness, and damping. All woofers use a Y-35 grade ferrite magnet motor with significantly higher performance than classic magnets, while the tweeters use a silk-impregnated fabric dome with oversized voice coils ventilated and damped with iron-fluid oil.

6000 Car Special Series

Vehicle-specific systems engineered for a factory-perfect fit and a far-from-factory sound.

The new speakers dedicated to Mercedes-Benz vehicles join the CS series, specially engineered to elevate sound performance to a completely new level — higher quality with better efficiency than the originals, with neodymium magnets, special voice coils, and a new high-performance motor in relation to the reduced space required.

These speakers are designed to integrate easily into many Mercedes-Benz vehicles without modifications. They have a shallow depth and include special adapters to match car spaces exactly, with connectors that match the car's original wiring for ultimate plug-and-play convenience.

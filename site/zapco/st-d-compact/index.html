Zapco

ST-D Compact Series

ST-D Compact — full Zapco power in a dramatically smaller footprint.

Over the last years Zapco has introduced more than pure high-end SQ competition amps — also a line of great-sounding, affordable amps called the Studio series. The Studio-D amps are full-range Class-D amps with great sound, a compact chassis, and crystal-clear power.

The ST-D Compact Series takes compactness further: chassis dimensions reduced to just 12.4 cm (W) × 5 cm (H). And no compromise on power — the ST-104D SQ III delivers up to 4 × 100 W into 4 Ω, the ST-105D adds a dedicated mono sub channel, and the ST-750D pumps 750 W mono. You no longer have to choose between sound quality, power, and available space.
